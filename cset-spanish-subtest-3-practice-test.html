Study Tips for Subtest 3

Use these alongside your practice questions.

🌎

Study Major Cultural Movements

Know modernismo, the Boom, and the Generation of '98. Be able to identify their key authors, themes, and historical context.

📜

Know the Literary Periods

From the Middle Ages through contemporary literature — memorize key works, authors, and the social forces that shaped each era.

✍️

Practice Composition Structures

Master argument organization: introduction with thesis, body with evidence and transitions, conclusion. Avoid calcos (false cognate structures from English).

🎙

Review SLA Theory

Krashen, Vygotsky's ZPD, Swain's Output Hypothesis, and Long's Interaction Hypothesis are all high-yield for Subtest 3 theory questions.

🎭

Study Hispanic Cultural Practices

Quinceañeras, Día de los Muertos, compadrazgo, fiestas patrias, and other cultural institutions appear in context questions.

🔊

Practice Aural Processing

Study top-down and bottom-up listening models. Know strategies for teaching listening comprehension in second-language classrooms.
