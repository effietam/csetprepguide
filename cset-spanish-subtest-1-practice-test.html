Study Tips for Subtest 1

Use these alongside your practice questions.

📖

Read the Official SMRs

Download the CSET Spanish Subject Matter Requirements from ctcexams.nesinc.com. Every question maps to a specific competency.

🧠

Master Subjunctive Triggers

Heavily tested. Learn the WEIRDO categories: Wishes, Emotion, Impersonal expressions, Requests, Doubt/Denial, Ojalá.

📰

Read Authentic Texts

Practice with Spanish newspaper articles, short stories, and essays to build reading speed and comprehension skills.

❌

Study False Cognates

Learn embarazado, sensible, librería, éxito, and others — they appear regularly in grammar and vocabulary questions.

🎙

Know Krashen's Theories

The Input Hypothesis (i+1) and Affective Filter are explicitly tested in the oral communication domain. Review them carefully.

✍️

Practice Written Responses

The real exam includes constructed-response questions. Practice writing short formal paragraphs in Spanish on educational topics.
